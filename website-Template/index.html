Our Mission

Create and develop a diverse learning and social environment for club members. Students can enhance their technical and interpersonal skills through interaction with other members, sharing experiences and knowledge
about technology. The club's efforts include fostering ethical and professional ideals.

Our club encompasses

  • Computer Systems Technology (CST)
  • Computer Engineering Technology (CET)
  • Electrical Engineering Technology (EET)
  • Telecommunications (TC)
  • The Club is open invitation for any one
    who interested in Computer technology.
