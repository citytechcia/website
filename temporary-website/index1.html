Computer Information Association

Welcome! Are you looking for a fun learning experience?

Computer Information Association (CIA) is a student organization that provides a creative and fun learning environment where City Tech students interested in technology and computers come together to explore their ideas. The club will plan and organize projects and activities, outside the classroom, with the purpose of enhancing technical skills and knoweledge. Programs are designed to create an environment of team work and to enhance interpersonal and social skills.
